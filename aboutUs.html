Background

In Tamil nadu history North India Tour Organizers play a role, as they form a bridge between North India and South India in exchanging the diverse cultures, practices of different people and states. They also facilitate pilgrims to visit various religious important places. These Organizers play a good role in bringing up All India Integral Co-Operation.

There are many such Organizers available and one of the blessed and liked by Pilgrims is Sri Maha Lakshmi Yathra Service. This organization was established by Late Brahma Shri S.V.D. Ayyer in 1967 and proud to say still running with same name for the third generation.

Sri Maha Lakshmi Yathra Service has successfully completed 52 years of its service and Stepping in to its 53rd year in its own building. The blessings of Jagadguru Sri Paramacharyal, Lord Sri Venkateswara have given us success. Many yathris, elders, well wishers, participated in many of our yathras, encouraged us and contributed to our success.
